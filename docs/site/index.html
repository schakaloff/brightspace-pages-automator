What it does

Checks nothing went missing

Compares your Brightspace course against the original Moodle course and reports anything that didn't survive the import.

Combines unit pages

Collects every topic page in a unit and merges them into one tidy, collapsible page — ready to publish.

Restyles pages with AI

Opens each page, restyles the content to the college's Brightspace theme, and saves it back — in batches.

Install in three steps

  1. Download the installer

    Click the download button above and save the file.

  2. Run it

    Double-click the downloaded file. If Windows shows a "protected your PC" screen, click More info, then Run anyway — that screen appears for any new app, it's normal.

  3. Open the app

    Find Brightspace Pages Automator in your Start menu, sign in to Brightspace once, and you're ready.
